# Lumina Engine

**Private settlement infrastructure for digital assets.**

Lumina Engine enables confidential financial transactions on blockchain infrastructure. The system allows users to trade, transfer, and settle assets without exposing positions, balances, or strategies to the public.

Traditional blockchains make every transaction visible. Lumina introduces private execution and settlement while maintaining verifiable integrity.

Our goal is simple:

**Bring the confidentiality of private banking to digital asset markets.**

## Core Concepts

### Confidential Execution

Transactions can be executed without revealing strategy, balances, or counterparty details to the public network.

### Encrypted Intents

Users submit encrypted instructions that can be executed without exposing their financial intent.

### Multi-Venue Settlement

Lumina routes execution across multiple blockchain venues depending on the privacy and liquidity requirements of a transaction.

### Verifiable Integrity

While transaction details remain private, cryptographic proofs ensure correctness and settlement finality.

## Architecture Overview
